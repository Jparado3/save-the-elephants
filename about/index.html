Save The Elephants

Why save the elephants?

Elephants play an important role in the enviroment. During dry seasons, elephants have a way in finding water by digging into the ground with their tusk, also providing water for other animals. Elephants create huge gaps in the vegetation they eat in. These gaps create new pathways for other animals use, allowing the terrain to open up more. In fact, their dung contains seeds from their past meals, enabling new grass and other trees to grow. This helps the ecosystem stay healthy.
